About Me

I grew up in Quang Tri, Vietnam, and I graduated from Quang Tri Town High School, achieving a remarkable GPA of 9.43/10 in grade 12.

Participating and winning top awards in several STEM Competitions and Informatics Olympiads during three years of high school, I am deeply committed to using artificial intelligence to improve accessibility and quality of life for people with disabilities. After building and leading several projects, I aspire to transform technical innovations into meaningful social impact.
